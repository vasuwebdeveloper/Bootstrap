
SuiteCommerce MyAccount is a self-service web portal where customers can manage account information, view recent transactions, pay invoices, and more. SCMA sites do not include Shopping and Checkout applications common to typical SuiteCommerce sites.


SCMA includes various features and information in the My Account area of your site:

Purchases area, including information on current and past purchases

Billing information, including account balances, invoices, transaction history, and printable statements

Account settings, such as email preferences, password management, etc.

Support case management

Quotes management
